

### Prerequisites

This is the list of things you need to replicate our paper and models
* LaTeX
* R
* Rstudio
* Rstan
* and following R packages
  * papaja
  * knitcitations
  * tidyverse
  * magrittr
  * ggplot2
  * car 
  * MASS
  * brms
  * xtable
  * ggpubr
  * languageR
  * gdata
